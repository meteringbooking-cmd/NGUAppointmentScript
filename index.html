Smart Meter Exchange Appointment

1. Opening & Introduction

Good morning/afternoon, my name is [Agent Name] and I'm calling you from [Company Name].

I'm calling today about your upcoming need to exchange your smart meter. We need to schedule this appointment now to make sure your metering system stays accurate and compliant.

2. Verification

To confirm I'm speaking with the account holder, could you please tell me the first line of your address and your postcode?

Thank you for confirming that, [Customer Name].

3. Appointment Details

The exchange appointment usually takes our engineer about 1 to 2 hours to complete. Please note that your power will need to be switched off for a short period, typically 30 to 45 minutes, for the work to be done safely.

We will need clear, safe, and easy access to where your current electricity and gas meters are located. It's helpful if the area is clutter-free before our engineer arrives.

The appointment will be conducted by one of our fully qualified and background-checked [Company Name] engineers.

4. Scheduling Options

I have two slots available next week: would Monday, [Date] or Thursday, [Date] work better for you?
If chosen:
Perfect. I can book that for [Day], [Date] in the morning (8 AM - 1 PM) slot.
If declined:
No problem at all. Let's look further out. How about Tuesday, [Date], in the afternoon (1 PM - 6 PM)?

5. Confirmation

Great. I have now booked your smart meter exchange for [Day of the week], [Date], in the [Morning/Afternoon] slot.

You will receive an email or letter confirmation with all these details, including the date, time window, and a contact number, within the next 24 hours.
